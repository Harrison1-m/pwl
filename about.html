About Us

Learn more about our journey, mission, and team

Our Story

Powerful Leather Works was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, custom stitch balls and sports equipment to athletes, schools, and teams across Kenya. What started as a small workshop in Nairobi has grown into a trusted name in the sports industry, known for our craftsmanship, durability, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Over the years, we’ve expanded our product line to include jerseys, sports gear, and training equipment, all designed to help athletes perform at their best. Our team of skilled artisans and designers work tirelessly to ensure every product meets our high standards.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower athletes and teams with high-quality, custom sports equipment that enhances performance, durability, and pride in the game. We are committed to craftsmanship, innovation, and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of custom sports equipment in Africa, recognized for our quality, reliability, and contribution to the growth of sports in our communities.
