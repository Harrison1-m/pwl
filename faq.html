Frequently Asked Questions

All Products Services Ordering Shipping Returns
What materials are your stitch balls made from? ⌄
Our stitch balls are handcrafted using high-quality, durable leather and synthetic materials, designed for both performance and longevity. Each ball is stitched by hand to ensure maximum durability and a consistent feel.
Can I customize the design of my stitch ball? ⌄
Yes! We offer full customization for stitch balls, including team logos, colors, and even player names. Our design team will work with you to create a ball that perfectly matches your team’s identity.
Do you offer jerseys and other sports gear? ⌄
Absolutely! In addition to stitch balls, we provide high-quality sports jerseys, training equipment, and protective gear. All our products can be customized to fit your team’s needs.
What customization services do you offer? ⌄
We offer a range of customization services, including logo printing, color matching, and personalized text on stitch balls, jerseys, and other gear. Our team will guide you through the process to ensure your products are exactly as you envision.
Do you provide repair services for sports equipment? ⌄
Yes, we offer repair and maintenance services for all types of sports equipment, including stitch balls, jerseys, and training gear. Our goal is to extend the lifespan of your equipment and keep it in top condition.
Can I order in bulk for my team or school? ⌄
Yes! We specialize in bulk orders for teams, schools, and retailers. Contact us for wholesale pricing and to discuss your specific needs.
How do I place an order? ⌄
You can place an order by contacting us via phone, email, or through our website’s contact form. Our team will guide you through the process, from selecting products to finalizing your customization options.
What payment methods do you accept? ⌄
We accept a variety of payment methods, including bank transfers, mobile money, and credit/debit cards. Our team will provide you with payment details once your order is confirmed.
How long does it take to process an order? ⌄
Processing times vary depending on the complexity of your order. Standard orders typically take 5-7 business days, while custom or bulk orders may take longer. We’ll provide a timeline when you place your order.
Do you offer shipping within Kenya? ⌄
Yes, we offer shipping throughout Kenya. Delivery times and costs depend on your location. Contact us for more details.
Can you ship internationally? ⌄
Currently, we primarily ship within Kenya. However, we may accommodate international orders on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us to discuss your needs.
How can I track my order? ⌄
Once your order is shipped, we’ll provide you with a tracking number so you can monitor its progress. You can also contact us for updates at any time.
What is your return policy? ⌄
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you’re not happy with your order, please contact us within 7 days of delivery to discuss returns or exchanges. Customized items may not be eligible for return.
How do I request a repair or replacement? ⌄
If your product needs repair or replacement, please contact us with your order details. We’ll guide you through the process and ensure your equipment is restored to top condition.
